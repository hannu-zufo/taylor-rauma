# Iron & Tide Tattoo — Design Brainstorm

## Three Design Approaches

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** American Traditional meets Brutalist Print

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, unapologetic boldness — thick borders, heavy ink-black frames, no softness
2. Asymmetric editorial layout — content blocks collide like a zine, not a brochure
3. Every element feels hand-pressed, like letterpress on aged paper
4. Flash sheet aesthetic — art displayed like physical sheets pinned to a wall

**Color Philosophy:**
Deep ink black (#0D0A07) as the ground. Aged parchment (#E8D9B5) for text fields. Crimson red (#C0392B) as the single accent — used sparingly like a stamp. No gradients. Flat, solid fills only.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Offset grid — sections don't align to a single column. Hero is full-bleed with a diagonal text block. Portfolio uses a masonry-style collage. Text blocks are rotated slightly or offset to feel hand-placed.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thick black border frames on cards and sections (4–6px solid)
2. Ink-stamp style labels for section headers (all caps, tracked out)
3. Horizontal rule dividers styled as thick brushstrokes

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Hover states reveal a red underline or border flash. Clicks feel weighted — slight scale-down on press. No smooth easing; snappy, mechanical transitions.

**Animation:**
Entrance animations are fast and directional (slide-in from left, 200ms). No parallax. Hover on portfolio images: slight desaturation lift. Cursor changes to crosshair over interactive elements.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Playfair Display" Black — for hero titles and section headers
- Body: "IBM Plex Mono" — for all body copy, giving a typeset/printed feel
- Labels: All-caps tracked "Bebas Neue" for nav and stamps
</idea>

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Pacific Northwest Gothic — Dark Coastal Americana

**Core Principles:**
1. Deep, moody atmosphere — the feeling of a Gastown alley at night, rain on cobblestones
2. Traditional tattoo iconography woven into the UI as decorative elements
3. Warm amber and gold accents against near-black backgrounds — candlelight in darkness
4. Generous vertical rhythm — the site breathes slowly, like a long exhale

**Color Philosophy:**
Background: near-black charcoal (#111010). Primary text: warm off-white (#F0EAD6). Accent: aged gold (#C9A84C). Secondary accent: deep crimson (#8B1A1A). The palette evokes old tattoo parlor walls, amber glass, and worn leather.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Full-width sections with strong vertical stacking. Hero is cinematic — full viewport height with centered typographic lockup. Portfolio uses a 3-column grid with gold-bordered hover states. Navigation is a fixed top bar with a hand-drawn logo mark.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin gold horizontal rules between sections
2. Decorative traditional tattoo motifs (anchors, swallows, roses) used as section dividers
3. Subtle paper/grain texture overlay across the entire site

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Smooth, deliberate transitions (400ms ease). Hover states glow softly in gold. Scroll-triggered fade-ins for each section. The experience is reverent, not flashy.

**Animation:**
Hero text fades in with a slow upward drift. Portfolio cards lift slightly on hover with a gold border glow. Section transitions use opacity + translateY (30px → 0). Parallax on hero background only.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Cormorant Garamond" Bold Italic — editorial, old-world gravitas
- Body: "Lato" Regular — clean and legible against dark backgrounds
- Accent labels: "Oswald" Medium — for nav items, CTAs, and metadata
</idea>

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Victorian Tattoo Parlour — Ornate & Theatrical

**Core Principles:**
1. Theatrical excess — ornamental borders, decorative flourishes, richly layered visual texture
2. The website as a cabinet of curiosities — each section is a display case
3. Deep jewel tones against black, like stained glass in a dark room
4. Typography as decoration — letterforms are part of the visual composition

**Color Philosophy:**
Background: near-black (#0C0C0E). Jewel accent: deep teal (#1A5C5A) and burgundy (#6B1A2A). Gold filigree (#B8962E) for ornamental details. The palette feels like a Victorian apothecary crossed with a tattoo flash book.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Centered, symmetrical compositions with strong vertical axis. Sections are framed by ornamental SVG borders. Portfolio is displayed in a symmetrical 2×3 grid. The overall feel is a museum catalog.

**Signature Elements:**
1. SVG ornamental corner flourishes on cards and section headers
2. Decorative drop caps on body text blocks
3. Wax seal / badge motifs for CTAs and social links

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Hover states reveal ornamental borders expanding outward. Buttons have a pressed-wax-seal feel. The entire experience is ceremonial — every interaction feels considered.

**Animation:**
Ornamental borders draw in on scroll (SVG stroke-dashoffset animation). Hero has a slow zoom-out on the background image. Cards rotate 1–2 degrees on hover. Entrance animations use staggered fade-ins.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Cinzel Decorative" — Roman-inspired, highly ornamental
- Body: "EB Garamond" — classical, scholarly, pairs beautifully with Cinzel
- Labels: "Cinzel" (non-decorative) for nav and metadata
</idea>

## Selected Approach: **Pacific Northwest Gothic — Dark Coastal Americana**

Deep, moody atmosphere with warm gold accents. Full-bleed cinematic sections. Traditional tattoo iconography as decorative elements. Cormorant Garamond for display, Lato for body, Oswald for labels.
